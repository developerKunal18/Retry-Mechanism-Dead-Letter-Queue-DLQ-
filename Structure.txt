🧠 Why This Is Important
Normally:
Producer
   ↓
Consumer

But what if processing fails?
Producer
   ↓
Queue
   ↓
Consumer
   ↓
❌ Failure

Using retries and a Dead Letter Queue:
Queue
 ↓
Consumer
 ↓
Retry 1
 ↓
Retry 2
 ↓
Retry 3
 ↓
Dead Letter Queue

Failed messages are preserved instead of being lost.
